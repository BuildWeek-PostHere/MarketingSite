Unsure where to post?

imPoster Syndrome is an app tailor made to save you time when looking to find where to ask your questions. Simply insert your post and we'll give you the most appropriate sub-reddit's to submit it to! To sign up and try it out click the button below to Get Started!

Get Started!

How it works?

We use machine learning to gather information from tons of posts every day. That data is then used to match keywords and phrases within your questions to generate a list of appropriate sub-reddits. That list is then sorted to give you the best possible choice for your questions.

The Future?

We're always aiming to improve our product with new features for users. In the future you will be able to manage posts and we'll automatically notify you if a new subreddit is made that better suits your post. You will also be able to submit your posts directly from the app, even to multiple sub-reddit's at once.
